Why most Vastu advice confuses you

Stop collecting tips. Learn to read your own home.

Most people gather scattered Vastu rules from videos and family advice — and end up more confused. Vastu Gurukul teaches the actual structure: how directions, zones, floor plans, numbers and remedies fit together so you can read a space yourself.

Common questions

Beginner doubts, answered.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?
Yes — the courses are built specifically for beginners, moms and homemakers. No prior Vastu knowledge is needed. You start from the very basics and learn step by step.
Are the classes live or recorded?
Classes are taught live online by Acharya Monica, so you can ask questions in real time. Every session is also recorded, so you can rewatch anytime if you miss a class.
What language are the courses in?
Classes are taught in a mix of Hindi and English so they're easy to follow for most learners across India.
Can I use this for my own home?
Absolutely — that's the whole point. You'll learn to read and correct your own home first, using simple remedies that need no demolition. Many students later go on to read for family and friends too.
